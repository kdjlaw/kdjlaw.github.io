New Jersey Lawyers Who See Ways to Maximize Damages for the Injured

We take pride in finding pathways to maximizing recovery for our clients that other lawyers might not see.

For example, in a 2002 case involving medical malpractice in a stillbirth, recovery was thought to be limited to a total of $300,000 under the rules of the New Jersey Property Liability Guaranty Association. We argued that there were actually two instances of malpractice in the case - negligence on the part of the obstetrician involved, and negligence by the certified midwife involved. As a result of our efforts, the amount of recovery was doubled to $600,000.
