New Jersey Attorneys Handling Wrongful Death Cases

Prematurely losing a loved one in a fatal accident is one of the most difficult situations a family will ever have to endure. Although nothing can change what has taken place, pursuing a wrongful death suit against the at-fault party may help the process of moving forward.

At the Westfield, New Jersey, law offices of Kessler, DiGiovanni & Jesuele, LLP, we understand that no amount of money can replace a family’s loss. However, a family certainly does not need to suffer economic hardship in addition to its grief. Although it is a time of mourning, it is very important to seek wise and prudent legal counsel to protect your family’s short term and long-term interests. Contact us to arrange a free consultation.

Seeking Financial Compensation for Your Loss

If someone dies as a result of the negligent or reckless conduct of another, the family may pursue a wrongful death lawsuit. Wrongful death claims often arise out of car accidents, construction accidents, or in cases of medical malpractice. Financial recovery can include compensation for lost earnings, as well as loss of companionship.
