New Jersey Injury Lawyers Handling Workers' Compensation Claims

When you go to work each day, you expect your workplace to be safe. Even when dangerous conditions exist, specific safety precautions should be in place to protect you from injury. Unfortunately, workplace injuries still occur at an alarming rate.

When an employee is hurt while performing work-related duties, New Jersey workers' compensation law governs from whom and how much an employee can recover.

At the Westfield, New Jersey, law offices of Kessler, DiGiovanni & Jesuele, LLP, we represent employees with workers' compensation concerns. If you were injured while on the job and feel you have not received all of the workers' compensation benefits to which you are entitled, we can help. Our attorneys have helped the injured and their families seek justice since 1966.

Contact a knowledgeable lawyer for a free consultation. We are available to meet with you in our office, in your home or in the hospital at a time that is convenient for you.

 

Seeking the Benefits You Deserve

Workers' compensation benefits may include payment of accident related medical expenses, payment of wages while you are recuperating from your injuries and an additional award if you are permanently disabled. We can help you get the compensation you need and deserve by effectively dealing with the following questions as they relate to New Jersey workers' compensation laws and guidelines:

  • Whether you were an employee at the time of the injury
  • Was the injury received while you were on the job
  • Was there an injury
  • What benefits are you entitled to
  • Was the injury permanent
  • Are you entitled to a permanency award

Our principal attorneys have more than 90 years of combined experience handling injury cases. We know how to deal with complex medical reports, and how to consult with medical experts to establish the nature and extent of a work-related injury.

In addition, our seasoned litigators will explore ways of establishing third party liability against a party other than your employer that may have caused or contributed to you workplace injury. This may entitle you to damages over and above workers' compensation benefits.

Put Our Workers' Compensation Experience to Work for You

When you hire the experts at Kessler, DiGiovanni & Jesuele, LLP to represent you, you can be sure that all avenues to maximize your recovery will be thoroughly explored and utilized.
