Motorcycle Accidents

New Jersey Lawyers Representing Victims of Motorcycle Accidents

For an avid motorcyclist, there are few things better than the open road â€” the wind whipping through their hair, the sun beating down upon them. Sadly, this invigorating experience can come to an untimely end when a speeding car or truck carelessly intercepts a motorcycle's path. Motorcycle accidents often result in extremely serious injuries, including spinal cord injuries, head injuries, traumatic brain injuries (TBIs), burns and broken bones. Some of these injuries prove fatal.

With more than 90 years of combined experience, the personal injury attorneys at Kessler, DiGiovanni & Jesuele, in Westfield, New Jersey, are dedicated to pursuing much-needed compensation for those injured in motorcycle accidents. The aftermath of a motorcycle accident can be overwhelming for victims and their families. Let us help during these difficult times.

Contact a knowledgeable lawyer for a free consultation. We are available to meet with you in our office, in your home, or in the hospital at a time that is convenient for you.

Bring an Expert on Board

Our attorneys have the experience and skill needed to explore every available source of compensation to cover medical bills, property damage, lost wages, and pain and suffering. In addition to their sharp-sightedness, they are not afraid to take on the largest adversaries in their pursuit of justice for motorcycle accident victims.

At Kessler, DiGiovanni & Jesuele, we are selective about the cases we take. In fact, we are committed to providing every client with customized and responsive representation. We always put forth the time and effort needed to truly understand the impact that a motorcycle accident has had on your life. Rest assured, you will always be kept updated on any developments in your case and we are always available to answer any questions that you may have.

You Have Nothing to Lose

We handle all personal injury cases on a contingency basis. This means you will not be charged attorney fees unless we are successful in recovering compensation for your injuries. Contact us to schedule a no cost, no obligation consultation to discuss how we may be of assistance.
