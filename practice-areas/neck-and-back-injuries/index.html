Neck and Back Injuries

Westfield, New Jersey, Neck and Back Injury Lawyers

The costs associated with disability due to neck or back injuries can be substantial. At the law office of Kessler, Digiovanni & Jesuele, our personal injury attorneys recover financial losses for victims of neck and back injuries. Regardless of whether you're facing short or long-term disability, we consult medical experts who can document and describe the scope and extent of your injuries. Since insurance companies are committed to reducing their costs, it's not unusual for them to claim a person's injuries stem from a pre-existing condition or are the result of other factors unrelated to an accident. Our lawyers are prepared for the technical and scientific demands of today's courtroom, preparing computer animation, charts, photos, and other information for jurors.

If you're facing an uncertain economic future due to a serious neck or back injury, contact the personal injury attorneys at the law office of Kessler, Digiovanni & Jesuele today. We provide free consultations in order to evaluate your case and discuss the options available to you for recovering financial damages.

Neck and Back Injuries that lead to Disability and Medical Costs

The law office of Kessler, Digiovanni & Jesuele represents clients in cases involving the following kinds of injuries:

  • Crushed vertebrae
  • Broken back
  • Whiplash
  • Herniated and bulging discs
  • Aggravation of degenerative spine disease
  • Spinal cord injury
  • Torn ligaments
  • Shoulder injury / dislocations
  • Paraplegia
  • Quadriplegia

Compensation for Pain and Suffering

Victims of neck and back injuries often face partial or total paralysis and the inability to perform common, every day tasks. Our attorneys think it's extremely important for jurors to understand how your injury has impacted you and your family. Towards that end, we create day-in-the-life videos when necessary, provide "before" and "after" photographs, and document how your life has been irrevocably changed by your injuries. We make sure jurors understand what your condition means for your children, your spouse, and you. We show your inability to do the things you always loved doing. We put a face to neck and back injuries and demand compensation for your pain and suffering.
