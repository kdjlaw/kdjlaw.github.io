Personal Injury

A Westfield, New Jersey, Law Firm With a History of Success

At the Westfield, New Jersey, law offices of Kessler, DiGiovanni & Jesuele, LLP, we represent individuals and families involved in personal injury and wrongful death cases caused by the negligence or recklessness of others. Rather than attempt to be "all things to all people," we've dedicated our resources and time to personal injury law.

Since our firm's establishment in 1966, we have worked to assist clients who find themselves facing an uncertain future due to a serious accident. In building our clients' cases, we work with investigators and a host of medical and scientific experts. Our efforts have resulted in significant financial recoveries for hundreds of clients.

If you or a loved one has been seriously injured in an accident, we can help. Contact our office to discuss your case with an experienced personal injury attorney.

Commitment, Dedication, and Resolve

The symbol for our law firm is the American Bald Eagle. The eagle is known for its excellent eyesight. We take pride in our vision, which we use to spot applicable theories of liability and to find ways to maximize compensation for our clients. Our record speaks for itself. We have successfully represented countless individuals and families in personal injury cases involving:

  • Motor Vehicle Accidents: car accidents, truck wrecks, motorcycle crashes
  • Dangerous Properties: slip and fall accidents, dog and animal bites
  • Use of Defective Products: fires and explosions, medication and drug injuries
  • Medical Malpractice: failure to diagnose, surgical errors, nursing home negligence
  • Industrial and Workplace Accidents: construction site accidents, third party liability

Insurance Companies Do Not Have Your Best Interests at Heart

Insurance companies want to limit their exposure to liability when a serious injury occurs. Through decades of combined experience, our attorneys have become very familiar with insurance company tactics, while protecting and preserving the rights, health, and future welfare of the citizens of New Jersey.

If you were seriously injured, you want experienced lawyers on your side who know how to get the compensation you need. Contact the experienced attorneys of Kessler, DiGiovanni & Jesuele, LLP. We offer a free consultation and do not charge fees unless we recover damages for you.
